## What Is Search Intent?

Search intent is **why** a user types a query. Google ranks content that matches intent.
There are 4 types:

| Intent Type | What the User Wants | Example Query |
|---|---|---|
| **Informational** | Learn something | "what is digital marketing" |
| **Navigational** | Find a specific site | "PixelPeak Digital Hyderabad" |
| **Commercial** | Research before buying | "best digital marketing agency Hyderabad" |
| **Transactional** | Ready to hire/buy | "hire SEO agency Hyderabad" |

## 📍 Local SEO Notes

- Always pair **service + city**: "SEO agency Hyderabad", not just "SEO agency"
- Mention Hyderabad-specific areas where natural: Hitech City, Gachibowli, Banjara Hills, Kondapur
- Use **"near me"** intent language: "looking for a digital marketing agency near Hitech City"
- Include **Google Business Profile** CTA at end of each blog
- Schema markup recommendation: `LocalBusiness` + `Article` schema on all blogs
